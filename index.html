Who are we?

Balkan Bites is bringing you the best all-natural Serbian-American cuisine on the Nashville scene! Local meats and veggies from right here in Tennessee are what go into each and every bite from this fantastic food truck. Burgers, brats, sarma, and so much more are on the menu to feed your need for the most magnificent meals you can find on the move on the streets of Nashville! Don't miss out, this is an eating experience you're sure to love!
